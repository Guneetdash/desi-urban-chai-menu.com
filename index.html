About

Hritika and Jarman are both young entrepreneurs who were looking to start a business together. They were inspired by the success of Desi Urban Chai and decided to open their own outlet in their hometown of Mohali. They were excited to bring the brand's signature flavors and ambiance to their community.

Desi Urban Chai is a well-known brand that has gained popularity for its unique blend of Indian and Western flavors in its tea offerings. The chain has a loyal following and has been expanding rapidly in recent years, with outlets in multiple cities across India
